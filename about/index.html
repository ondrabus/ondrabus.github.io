About

I am a Developer Evangelist at Kentico. My web development experience started many years ago with creating websites for friends. Later I took a summer job as a developer intern and once I finished my master studies I went through developer and customer-success related jobs to my current position. I enjoy working with the development community and educating developers, learning and utilizing new technologies as well as listening to others presenting their successes.

Kentico

I started working at Kentico in 2015 as a Solution Architect. Coming from a partner agency where I was employed as a developer, I had a unique insight into developers' day to day work and their typical problems. That helped me to provide a great service to our clients and partners as we were talking the same language and it was easy for me to understand their use-cases.

Being also involved with pre-sales support and consulting I was able to help point many of our clients' projects to a way to success. They include Sazka, KB, EVN, DHL Freight, Allergan and many more.

Developer Evangelism

Being a Solution Architect was not just about coming up with solutions to client's problems. Great portion of my job was educating others. Not just about Kentico CMS, but web development in general. That has led me to my current position where I talk to developers from all over the world about their problems and daily struggles and try to help them any way I can.

I published a lot of content about static sites on Medium, Kentico Cloud blog and Gatsby. I try to keep exploring new technologies and run a Twitch session every two weeks where I present them (or at least those that I am familiar enough with :-)). Every now and then I also attend conferences either as a speaker or attendee.


Bus driving

Public transportation has always inspired me. During my studies at Brno University of Technology I was not a typical A-student :-). I had to extend my bachelor's studies for one year during which I used to work as a bus driver full-time. Nowadays I just do it for fun.

I used to drive long distances, mainly within Czech republic. These days you may find me driving around Brno, my home town, either in evenings or during weekend nights. Doing it for fun means not every weekend, I still like to enjoy a beer or two and that does not go well with driving :-)
